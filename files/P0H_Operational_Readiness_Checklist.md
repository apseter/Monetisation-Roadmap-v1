## P0H.02: TERIGNOTA PRODUCT EXPANSION MONITORING

**Why:** Terignota adding a modular liner system is our single highest competitive risk. We need an early warning system.

**Deliverable:** Monitoring system (15-minute setup, 5 minutes/month maintenance)

**Setup steps:**

1. **Follow on Instagram:** @terignota — enable post notifications (bell icon)
2. **Follow on TikTok:** @terignota — enable notifications
3. **Sign up for email list:** Go to terignota.com → footer → email signup. You'll get product launch announcements.
4. **Set Google Alert:** Go to google.com/alerts → create alert for "Terignota new product" + "Terignota modular" + "Terignota liner"
5. **Monthly website check:** Visit terignota.com on the 1st of each month. Screenshot product page. Note any new products.
6. **Reddit monitoring:** Search r/trailrunning for "Terignota" monthly. Note any mentions of new products.

**What to watch for:**
- Any mention of "detachable," "modular," "snap-in," "removable liner" in their marketing
- New product launches (especially shorts with separate liner options)
- Price increases (they're at $29 — if they move to $35+, they may be adding features)
- International expansion (currently US-only — if they enter EU, note this immediately)

**Trigger for action:** If Terignota announces anything modular, immediately escalate to P0F risk register and accelerate our timeline.

**Estimated time:** 15 minutes setup + 5 minutes/month

## P0H.03: CURRENCY IMPACT MODELING

**Why:** Our pricing is locked at $35 / €33 / £28 for the outer short, but EUR/USD and GBP/USD fluctuations directly affect gross margin on EU/UK sales. A 10% EUR/USD swing could materially change profitability.

**Deliverable:** Simple sensitivity table in Google Sheets (feeds into P7A Financial Planning)

**Model structure:**

| Exchange Rate | EUR Price | Revenue (USD equiv.) | FOB Cost | Shipping | 3PL | Gross Margin | GM% |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| EUR/USD 0.95 | €50 system | $47.50 | $10.00 | $5.00 | $3.00 | $29.50 | 62% |
| EUR/USD 1.00 | €50 system | $50.00 | $10.00 | $5.00 | $3.00 | $32.00 | 64% |
| EUR/USD 1.05 | €50 system | $52.50 | $10.00 | $5.00 | $3.00 | $34.50 | 66% |
| EUR/USD 1.10 | €50 system | $55.00 | $10.00 | $5.00 | $3.00 | $37.00 | 67% |
| EUR/USD 1.15 | €50 system | $57.50 | $10.00 | $5.00 | $3.00 | $39.50 | 69% |

*Repeat for GBP/USD with £43 system price.*

**Key insight:** Since FOB and most costs are in USD, and revenue is in EUR/GBP, a weaker dollar (higher EUR/USD) improves margin, and a stronger dollar hurts margin. At EUR/USD 0.95 (strong dollar), our system margin drops to ~62%. At 1.10, it rises to ~67%.

**Hedging consideration:** At our volume (<$500K year 1), formal currency hedging isn't cost-effective. Instead: (1) price in round EUR/GBP numbers, (2) review pricing quarterly vs exchange rates, (3) build 5% currency buffer into margin targets.

**Estimated time:** 1 hour to build in Google Sheets
**Status:** ☐ Not Started

## P0H.04: EU LAST-MILE SHIPPING COST ESTIMATES

**Why:** P0G covers Karachi→EU warehouse freight but NOT the warehouse→customer doorstep delivery cost. This directly affects the free shipping threshold and conversion rate.

**Deliverable:** Shipping cost matrix by country + recommended free shipping threshold

**Research needed (include in EU 3PL quote request from P0G.07):**

Ask each 3PL (ShipBob EU, Byrd, Huboo) for:

1. **Per-order pick + pack fee:** Usually €1.50-3.00 per order
2. **Last-mile delivery cost by country:**
   - UK: £2.50-4.00 (Royal Mail/Evri from UK warehouse)
   - France: €3.50-5.00 (Colissimo/Mondial Relay)
   - Germany: €3.50-5.00 (DHL Germany/Hermes)
   - Spain: €4.00-6.00
   - Italy: €4.00-6.00
   - Rest of EU: €5.00-8.00
3. **Tracked vs untracked pricing**
4. **Express/next-day option pricing**
5. **Return shipping label cost** (for P0H.05)

**Free shipping threshold analysis:**

| Threshold | Products Qualifying | Customer Impact | Our Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| Free on ALL orders | Everything | Maximum conversion | Absorb €3-6/order |
| Free above €40 | System bundle (€50), Double Liner (€65) | Good — encourages system bundle | ~€3-5 on qualifying orders |
| Free above €50 | System (€50), Double Liner (€65), His&Hers (€90) | OK — nudges toward bundles | ~€3-5 on qualifying orders |
| Flat €5 on all orders | Everything | Fair but adds friction | Customer pays most of cost |

**Recommendation:** Free shipping above €50. This captures the system bundle (our primary product) and all bundles, while charging €5 flat rate on the outer-only order (€33). This incentivizes the system bundle, which has higher margin.

**Estimated time:** 2-3 hours (included in 3PL quote request)
**Status:** ☐ Not Started

## P0H.05: EU RETURN LOGISTICS

**Why:** EU consumer law mandates 14-day unconditional returns for online purchases. We need a return address in the EU/UK and a returns processing workflow.

**Deliverable:** Returns process flowchart + 3PL returns pricing

**Key requirements:**

1. **Return address must be in EU/UK** — customers will NOT return to Pakistan. This is non-negotiable for consumer trust and legal compliance.
2. **Return processing:** 3PL receives return → inspects → restocks (if good condition) or writes off (if worn/damaged)
3. **Return shipping cost:** We can either (a) offer free returns (absorb cost, ~€3-5 per return), or (b) customer pays return shipping (legal in EU/UK but reduces trust). Recommendation: Customer pays return shipping (standard for DTC brands), but we offer free exchanges (exchange-first policy from P0F).

**Include in 3PL quote request:**

Ask ShipBob EU / Byrd / Huboo:
- Do you process returns? (Y/N)
- Per-return processing fee? (Usually €1-3)
- Do you inspect and restock? (Y/N)
- Return label generation? (Automated or manual?)
- Return tracking for customers?
- Average return turnaround time?

**Return rate benchmarks (DTC apparel):**
- Industry average online apparel return rate: 20-30%
- Trail running shorts (performance fit): Expected 10-15% (lower than fashion because buyers research more)
- Most common return reasons: Size/fit (60-70%), didn't meet expectations (15-20%), defective (5-10%)
- Mitigation: Detailed size guide (cm + inch), fit photos on diverse body types, fit quiz (Shopify app)

**Estimated time:** Included in 3PL quote request (P0G.07). Returns questions add 30 minutes.
**Status:** ☐ Not Started
