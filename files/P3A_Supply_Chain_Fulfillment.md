## P3A.1: FREIGHT FROM PAKISTAN TO UK WAREHOUSE

### P3A.1.1: Air Freight (Recommended for 300 Units)

For a shipment of ~1-2 CBM and 30-50 kg, air cargo via a consolidator is the clear winner over sea freight. The cost difference is marginal (£100-200), but air saves 4-5 weeks of transit time.

| Route | Transit | Cost (50 kg) | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Air consolidator (Lahore → UK)** | **5-8 days** | **£150-350** | **Recommended** |
| DHL Express | 2-3 days | £400-750 | Faster but expensive |
| Sea LCL (Karachi → Felixstowe) | 30-45 days | £250-550 | Too slow for launch |

### P3A.1.2: Pakistan-UK Freight Forwarders

| Forwarder | Speciality | Contact | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **DFS Worldwide** | UK-based, Pakistan textile specialist | dfsworldwide.com | Good for first-timers |
| **Yes2Ship** | IATA-accredited, daily departures from Sialkot/Lahore | Tel: 0208 754 9000 | Air freight specialist |
| **Smile Cargo** | Budget air cargo | smilecargo.co.uk | From £4.99-7.50/kg |
| **Freightos** | Digital platform, compare 75+ forwarders | freightos.com | Good for price comparison |

### P3A.1.3: Landed Cost Per Unit (Air Freight)

| Component | Cost (GBP) |
|---|---|
| Air freight (50 kg, consolidator) | £150-350 |
| Pakistan origin charges (docs, handling) | £30-60 |
| UK customs broker fee | £100-200 |
| Import duty (DCTS Enhanced = **0%**) | £0 |
| Import VAT (20%, reclaimable via PVA) | ~£540 (net **£0**) |
| UK delivery (airport → warehouse) | £30-80 |
| Cargo insurance (~0.5% of value) | £12-15 |
| **Total out-of-pocket** | **£320-700** |
| **Per-unit landed cost** | **£1.07-2.33** |

### P3A.1.4: Postponed VAT Accounting (PVA) — Critical

As a VAT-registered UK Ltd, use Postponed VAT Accounting (PVA). Declare import VAT on your VAT return as both output and input tax simultaneously — **net cash impact is zero.** Tick the PVA box on the customs declaration.

If you're NOT yet VAT-registered, register voluntarily before the first import. Otherwise the ~£540 import VAT becomes a sunk cost until your first VAT return.

## P3A.2: UK IMPORT — DCTS DUTY-FREE ACCESS & CUSTOMS

### P3A.2.1: DCTS Enhanced Preferences (UK)

Pakistan textiles enter the UK at **0% duty** under the Developing Countries Trading Scheme (DCTS), saving 12% versus MFN rates.

**Documentation for UK imports:**
- Place an origin declaration on the commercial invoice stating Pakistan preferential origin
- For consignments under **£5,400** (yours will be ~£2,400), any exporter can self-certify — no "approved exporter" status needed
- Double-stage transformation rule: fabric must be both woven/knitted AND cut-and-sewn in Pakistan — met if your Lahore/Sialkot factory handles full CMT

### P3A.2.2: EU GSP+ via REX System

**Documentation for EU imports:**
- Pakistani manufacturer registers with TDAP (Trade Development Authority of Pakistan) to obtain a REX number
- Registration is one-time and free
- For consignments under **€6,000**, a Statement on Origin can be made without REX registration
- Statement on Origin goes on the invoice, references REX number (format: PKXXXXXXXXXX)

### P3A.2.3: Origin Rule Warning

**If you ship all inventory to UK first, then forward to EU customers, the goods LOSE preferential origin status.** EU customs would charge 12% MFN duty on Pakistan-origin goods transshipped via UK.

**For initial launch:** Shipping everything from UK is acceptable given low EU volumes (duty absorbed).
**When EU hits 100+ orders/month:** Ship a separate consignment direct from Pakistan to EU warehouse to preserve 0% GSP+ rate.

### P3A.2.4: HS Code Classification

Your shorts likely classify under:
- **HS 6203.43** — men's/boys' woven shorts, synthetic fibres
- **HS 6103.43** — knitted equivalent
- **HS 6204.63 / 6104.63** — women's equivalents

Get a **Binding Tariff Information (BTI)** ruling from HMRC before your first shipment to confirm.

## P3A.7: EU FULFILLMENT FROM UK + IOSS

### P3A.7.1: UK-to-EU Shipping Costs

| Carrier | Cost to France | Speed | Tracking |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Evri Europe Standard (via Parcel2Go)** | **£6.89** | 5-7 days | Basic |
| Royal Mail International Tracked | £8.50-10.50 | 5-7 days | Full |
| DHL Express | £15-30 | 2-4 days | Full |

### P3A.7.2: IOSS — Non-Negotiable for EU Sales

Without IOSS, French customers face €8-15 surprise charges at delivery + customs delays. With IOSS, customer pays total price at checkout — no surprises.

**How IOSS works:**
1. Register via EU intermediary (~€100-300/year through VAT Digital or Avalara)
2. At checkout, Shopify Markets charges destination-country VAT (France 20%, Germany 19%)
3. Your IOSS number goes on the customs label
4. EU customs clears parcel without additional charges for goods under €150

### P3A.7.3: Should You Pre-Position Stock in EU?

**No — not at 300 units.** Per-parcel cross-border cost (£6.89-10.50) is cheaper than maintaining a second warehouse until EU orders exceed **100 per month consistently.**

## P3A.8: FRANCE-SPECIFIC DELIVERY

### P3A.8.1: French Customer Expectations

French customers strongly prefer **pickup point delivery** (point relais) over home delivery. Key networks: Colissimo (La Poste), Mondial Relay, Chronopost relay points.

### P3A.8.2: Recommended France Carrier

**Asendia** — joint venture of La Poste and Swiss Post. Offers e-PAQ product range at £5-10 for lightweight parcels with DDP options and paperless IOSS customs clearance. Via Sendcloud, access Colissimo and Mondial Relay rates directly for a familiar local experience.

## P3A.9: JULY 2026 EU DE MINIMIS CHANGE

### P3A.9.1: What's Changing

The EU is abolishing the €150 customs duty exemption. From **1 July 2026**, a flat **€3 customs duty** applies to ALL low-value imports, plus national handling fees (France €5, Netherlands €2).

### P3A.9.2: Impact on Your Business

Even with IOSS, every UK→EU parcel will attract these charges. For a €33 pair of shorts to France: approximately **€8 additional cost** (€3 duty + €5 French handling).

**Action:** Factor into EU pricing NOW. Either absorb into product price (raise EU prices by €3-5) or add to shipping charges. Only 3.5 months away.

## P3A.10: RETURNS MANAGEMENT

### P3A.10.1: Expected Return Rate

Athletic shorts: **15-25% return rate.** Sizing is the dominant reason (50-60% of returns).

### P3A.10.2: Cost Per Return

| Component | UK Return | EU Return |
|---|---|---|
| Return shipping label | £2.50-4.00 | €8-15 (customer-paid) |
| Processing labor (inspect, 10-15 min) | £2.00-3.00 | Same |
| Restocking (new polybag) | £0.50-1.00 | Same |
| Write-off risk (10-20% unsellable) | £3.00-6.00 amortized | Same |
| Platform software (amortized) | £0.25-0.50 | Same |
| **Total per return** | **£8.25-14.50** | **£10-18+** |

At 20% return rate on £35 average order, returns consume ~£2.00/order sold when amortized — about 5-7 percentage points of margin.

### P3A.10.3: UK Returns Process

1. Customer requests return via email or self-serve portal
2. You email/text a pay-on-use return label (Evri or Royal Mail)
3. Customer drops at ParcelShop or Post Office
4. You receive, inspect within 14 days
5. Restock if unworn (no sweat marks, pilling, odour, tags intact)
6. Write off if worn
7. Issue refund to original payment method

### P3A.10.4: EU Returns

Accept returns to UK address with customer paying return postage (stated in T&Cs). As EU volume grows, consider ZigZag Global or Global24 for local EU return addresses.

### P3A.10.5: Returns Technology

| App | Cost | Returns Included | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Shopify Self-Serve Returns** | Free | Unlimited | Launch (0-5 returns/month) |
| **AfterShip Returns** | $23/month | 60 ($0.08 extra) | 5-60 returns/month |
| ReturnGO | $23/month | 20 ($1.25 extra) | Exchange-first strategy |
| Loop Returns | $155/month | Unlimited | Overkill at this scale |

**Start with Shopify Self-Serve Returns (free). Upgrade to AfterShip Returns at $23/month once returns exceed 5/month.**

### P3A.10.6: Minimizing Returns

Invest in a detailed size guide with actual body measurements, model measurements, and fit descriptions for both outer and liner. Install Kiwi Sizing (free) for an interactive fit quiz. Brands with strong size guides see **25-35% fewer size-related returns.**

## P3A.12: INVENTORY MANAGEMENT & REORDER TIMING

### P3A.12.1: The Bundle Inventory Problem

Install **Simple Bundles** ($15/month) on Shopify. When a bundle sells, it deducts from each component SKU. Without this, bundles don't decrement component inventory — creating phantom availability.

### P3A.12.2: SKU Structure

| Product | SKU Format | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Outer short | `OS-[COLOR]-[SIZE]` | OS-BLK-M |
| Liner | `LN-CMP-[SIZE]` | LN-CMP-M |
| Bundle | `BN-[COLOR]-CMP-[SIZE]` | BN-BLK-CMP-M |

### P3A.12.3: Reorder Action Plan

| Week | Action |
|---|---|
| Week 1-2 | Monitor daily sales velocity. Gather sizing feedback. |
| **Week 2-3** | **If selling >3 units/day, TRIGGER REORDER IMMEDIATELY.** |
| Week 4-6 | Continue selling. Production begins in Pakistan. |
| Week 8-12 | Risk of stockout. Deploy "Back In Stock" app. |
| Week 10-14 | Batch 2 arrives (if reordered at Week 2-3). |

**Soft launch first** (email list + social only, before paid ads) to gauge demand and calibrate reorder timing.

## P3A KEY INSIGHT

> **The first 300 units demand capital efficiency over operational sophistication.** Air-freight from Lahore, self-fulfill from home, ship via Evri at £2.62, handle returns manually. This keeps total supply chain under £7 per UK order while preserving full control over the unboxing experience that builds brand equity in the critical first months. Resist splitting inventory between UK and EU until EU orders consistently clear 100/month.
