## THE BRAND

We are launching a direct-to-consumer trail running shorts brand, manufactured in Pakistan, selling primarily to UK and EU trail runners. Our hero product is a **modular trail short system** — an outer short ($35) with a detachable compression liner ($18) that snaps in and out = $53 complete system. We compete against Path Projects ($105+ system, men only) at half the price, with women's sizing from launch and XS-3XL inclusivity.

**Brand personality:** Premium quality at accessible pricing. Authentic, not flashy. Trail-tested, not fashion-forward. Inclusive by design, not as an afterthought. European sensibility (UK-first launch, then France).

**Target customer:** Trail runners aged 25-45, running 15-50+ miles per week, who care about gear performance but resist paying $80-100+ for shorts. Both men and women. Frustrated with liners that chafe, phone pockets that bounce, and brands that only make men's sizes.

## WHAT WE NEED

### Deliverable 1: PRIMARY LOGO (Wordmark)
- Clean, modern wordmark of the brand name: **[NAME]**
- Must work at all sizes: website header, social avatar (small), product label, embroidery
- Design for **single-color reproduction** (looks good in all-black, all-white, and brand colors)
- Must work on both dark and light backgrounds
- Provide in: SVG, PNG (transparent), AI/EPS

### Deliverable 2: ICON MARK (Secondary)
- Simple geometric icon that can be used as:
  - Instagram/TikTok profile picture (circle crop)
  - Favicon (16×16 px legibility)
  - Embroidery on product waistband (1" width maximum)
  - Standalone brand stamp
- Should relate to trail/mountain/modular concept without being literal
- Must work at very small sizes (simplicity is key)
- Provide in: SVG, PNG (transparent), AI/EPS

### Deliverable 3: COLOR PALETTE
- **Primary foundation:** Deep slate/charcoal (not pure black — warmer)
- **Secondary:** Warm sand, sage green, or stone
- **Accent color:** ONE distinctive color that becomes ownable — suggest burnt orange, deep teal, terracotta, or similar earth tone
- Provide: HEX, RGB, CMYK, and Pantone codes for each color
- Show palette applied to: website mockup, social post, product tag

### Deliverable 4: TYPOGRAPHY SYSTEM
- Heading font + body font pairing
- Prefer: humanist or geometric sans-serif (similar to Inter, DM Sans, Manrope)
- Must be available as Google Font (free) OR provide licensing info if paid
- Show hierarchy: H1, H2, H3, body, caption, button text

### Deliverable 5: BRAND GUIDELINES (1-2 pages minimum)
- Logo usage rules (minimum size, clear space, do's and don'ts)
- Color application rules
- Typography hierarchy
- Photography style direction (3-5 reference photos showing the right "feel")

## AESTHETIC DIRECTION

### What we ARE:
- Earth-toned, warm, authentic (like Path Projects, NNormal, Tracksmith)
- Clean and modern but not sterile
- Premium-feeling but approachable (not luxury-coded like Satisfy or District Vision)
- Outdoor/trail — evokes mountains, terrain, natural landscapes
- Inclusive — brand should feel welcoming to ALL body types and genders

### What we are NOT:
- Neon/bright/bold (not Janji or Ciele territory)
- Ultra-minimalist monochrome (not On Running or Satisfy territory)
- Generic "sporty" (not Nike/Adidas aesthetic)
- Vintage/retro (not Tracksmith's collegiate vibe)
- Aggressive/extreme (not "beast mode" energy)

### Reference brands (for visual feel, not to copy):
1. **Path Projects** — earth tones, clean, trail-focused (our closest competitor visually)
2. **NNormal** — European outdoor aesthetic, premium but accessible
3. **Patagonia** — authentic, environmentally conscious, trustworthy
4. **Aesop** (non-running) — warm minimal, premium with earth tones

### Reference colors we like:
- The warm charcoal of Path Projects
- The sage/forest tones of Patagonia's trail line
- The burnt orange accents of NNormal
- Avoid: bright blue, bright red, pure black, pure white
