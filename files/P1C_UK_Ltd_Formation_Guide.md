## STEP 2: COMPANIES HOUSE ID VERIFICATION (Day 1-3)

Since November 2025, all directors must verify identity. Two options:

**Option A: GOV.UK One Login App (Fastest)**
1. Download "GOV.UK ID Check" app on phone
2. Create a GOV.UK One Login account
3. Scan biometric passport (NFC chip reading)
4. Take a selfie for facial matching
5. Verification usually completes in minutes

**Option B: Via Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP)**
- Your formation agent (if they're registered as an ACSP) can verify your ID
- They'll need your passport scan + proof of address
- Takes 1-3 business days

**Your company is legally formed as soon as Companies House processes it (typically same day or next business day). ID verification can happen in parallel.**

## STEP 3: RECEIVE COMPANY DOCUMENTS (Day 1-2)

You'll receive:
- **Certificate of Incorporation** — your company number (8 digits)
- **Memorandum of Association**
- **Articles of Association** (standard Model Articles are fine)
- **Share Certificate** (1 share of £1)

Save these digitally. You'll need the company number for everything that follows.

## STEP 4: OPEN WISE BUSINESS ACCOUNT (Day 2-5)

**Why Wise:** Opens fully remotely, no UK address required for the account holder, multi-currency (GBP + EUR + USD), provides UK sort code + account number needed for Shopify Payments and HMRC.

1. Go to **wise.com/business** → "Open a Business Account"
2. Select "Limited Company" → enter Companies House number
3. Upload director's passport (same one used for Companies House)
4. Upload proof of address (utility bill, bank statement — can be Pakistani address)
5. Pay one-time setup fee: **£45-50**
6. Account typically verified in **1-3 business days**

**What you get:**
- GBP account: sort code + account number (appears as UK domestic account)
- EUR account: IBAN (appears as EU domestic account)
- USD account: routing + account number
- Debit card: Wise business debit card (optional, for expenses)
- Multi-currency conversion at mid-market rate + small fee

**Alternative if Wise is slow:** Revolut Business (£25/month) or Tide (free, UK-only features).

## STEP 5: REGISTER FOR CORPORATION TAX WITH HMRC (Day 3-14)

**Must be done within 3 months of starting to trade.**

1. Go to **gov.uk/register-for-corporation-tax**
2. You'll need: company number, company name, date of incorporation, registered office address, date you started trading (or expect to start), SIC code, director details
3. Submit online — takes 15-20 minutes
4. HMRC will post a letter with your **Unique Taxpayer Reference (UTR)** — takes 1-2 weeks (sent to registered office address → formation agent forwards to you)

**Corporation tax rate:** 19% on profits up to £50,000. 25% on profits above £250,000. In between is marginal relief. For Year 1, you're almost certainly in the 19% bracket.

## STEP 6: REGISTER FOR UK VAT (Day 5-30)

**This is MANDATORY before your first sale.** As an overseas seller (you're based in Pakistan, selling goods shipped from outside UK with value ≤£135), you must register for VAT — there is no minimum turnover threshold.

1. Go to **gov.uk/register-for-vat**
2. Select "You are a non-established taxable person" (NETP) or simply register as the UK Ltd
3. You'll need: company number, UTR (from Step 5), bank account details (Wise sort code + account number), estimated taxable turnover
4. Processing time: **2-4 weeks** for VAT number

**VAT rate:** 20% standard rate on all products. Charge VAT-inclusive prices to UK customers. File VAT returns quarterly via Making Tax Digital compatible software (FreeAgent, Xero, or QuickBooks — accountant will advise).

**How it works with Shopify:** Enter your VAT number in Shopify Settings → Taxes → UK. Shopify automatically calculates and displays VAT-inclusive prices. You collect VAT from customers at checkout and remit to HMRC quarterly.

---

## STEP 7: OBTAIN UK EORI NUMBER (Day 5-30)

1. Go to **gov.uk/eori** → "Get an EORI number"
2. Usually linked to your VAT registration — format: GB + VAT number + 000
3. Free, usually issued same time as VAT number
4. Required for all customs declarations when importing goods to UK

## STEP 8: ACTIVATE SHOPIFY PAYMENTS (Day 14-30)

Once you have:
- UK Ltd company number ✓
- Wise Business bank account (sort code + account number) ✓
- UK registered address ✓
- Director details verified ✓

1. In Shopify: Settings → Payments → Shopify Payments → Activate
2. Enter: business legal name (your Ltd company name), company number, registered address, director details, bank details (Wise sort code + account number)
3. Shopify verifies in 1-2 business days
4. Transaction rate: **2.9% + 30p** on Basic plan (no additional Shopify transaction fee)

## STEP 10: REGISTER FOR EU IOSS (When Ready for France/EU)

**Not needed until you start selling to EU consumers.** Can defer to Week 8-10.

IOSS (Import One-Stop Shop) allows you to collect EU VAT at point of sale so customers don't get surprise charges. Required for consignments ≤€150 (your entire product range).

**Options:**
- **SimpleVAT:** €49/month — automated IOSS registration + filing
- **Taxually:** €50-200/month — full VAT compliance service
- **Via your UK accountant** — some firms offer IOSS intermediary services

Register via an intermediary in any EU member state (Ireland and Netherlands are most common for English-speaking businesses).
